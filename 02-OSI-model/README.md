# 📶 OSI
 **OSI** stands for Open Systems Interconnection.  
 It's a theoretical framework of 7-layer model.

 | Layer | TCP/IP | OSI | Data Unit | Hardware | Protocols |
|-------|--------|-----|-----------|----------|-----------|
| 7 | Application | Application | Data | Gateway | HTTP, FTP, SMTP , Telnet|
| 6 | Application | Presentation | Data | Gateway redirector | TLS, SSL |
| 5 | Application | Session | Data | Gateway | NetBIOS , SQL , DNS|
| 4 | Transport | Transport | Segment | Gateway| TCP, UDP , SSH-2|
| 3 | Internet | Network | Packet | Router | IP, ICMP , NAT , DHCP|
| 2 | Link | Data Link | Frame | Switch , Bridge | Ethernet, ARP |
| 1 | Link | Physical | Bits | Hub, Cable | DSL , SONET |
